NTA Digital develops interactive science and technology learning materials for Swedish schools as part of the Naturvetenskap och Teknik för Alla (NTA) programme. Working with educators, researchers, and subject matter experts, NTA Digital creates engaging digital experiences that help students explore complex scientific concepts through simulations, visualizations, experiments, and interactive learning activities.
