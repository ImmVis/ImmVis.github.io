Stenastiftelsen is a prominent Swedish philanthropic foundation established in 1996 to honor the legacy of shipowner Sten A. Olsson. The foundation is dedicated to promoting research and culture, providing substantial grants to outstanding individuals and innovative projects primarily within the Gothenburg region and western Sweden. By supporting a diverse range of initiatives across the humanities, medicine, technology, and the arts, Stenastiftelsen aims to foster creative environments, elevate academic excellence, and make groundbreaking science and cultural experiences accessible to the wider public.
