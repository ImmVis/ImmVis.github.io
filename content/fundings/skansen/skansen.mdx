Skansen is the world’s oldest open-air museum and zoo, located in Stockholm, and serves as a premier venue for bringing Swedish cultural heritage and natural history to life. Founded in 1891, the foundation is dedicated to public education, conservation, and experiential learning. As a key experiential partner, Skansen collaborates with forward-thinking organizations to integrate interactive, modern exhibits within its historic and scientific spaces—such as the Baltic Sea Science Center—bridging the gap between historical traditions and future-facing scientific discovery for millions of annual visitors.
