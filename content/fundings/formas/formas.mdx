Formas is a government research council dedicated to funding pioneering research and innovation that addresses global environmental and societal challenges. Operating under the Ministry of Climate and Enterprise, Formas promotes sustainable development through strategic funding, comprehensive policy analysis, and rigorous project evaluation. The Council focuses on three core areas: the environment, agricultural sciences, and spatial planning. By funding multi-year national research programs and driving cross-sector collaborations between academia, industry, and public authorities, Formas acts as a crucial catalyst for Sweden's transition toward a resilient, climate-neutral, and sustainable future.
