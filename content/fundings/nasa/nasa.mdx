NASA is the United States’ federal agency responsible for civilian space exploration, aeronautics research, and scientific discovery. Beyond its iconic space missions, NASA actively invests in open science and global public engagement through its Science Activation program. As a primary strategic partner and funder of the OpenSpace project, NASA provides both critical grant funding and direct access to its vast, authentic planetary and astrophysical datasets. This collaboration allows the open-source software to accurately visualize the known universe, turning complex space mission data into immersive, interactive educational experiences for planetariums, classrooms, and audiences worldwide.
