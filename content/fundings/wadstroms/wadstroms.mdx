Stig Wadströms Stiftelse is a Swedish philanthropic foundation established in 2018 through the legacy of Norrköping businessman and asset manager Stig Wadström. The foundation is dedicated to promoting education, scientific progress, and research that actively benefits Sweden and its inhabitants on both a local and national level. It maintains an exceptionally close partnership with Linköping University (LiU)—particularly Campus Norrköping—funding major strategic initiatives such as pioneering research into electronic medicines and the development of the Wadströms Exploranation Laboratory. By converting a historic textile-industry fortune into modern scientific capital, the foundation provides vital grants, student scholarships, and unique academic meeting spaces to drive cutting-edge innovation and public engagement with science.
