The Marcus and Amalia Wallenberg Foundation is a prominent Swedish philanthropic organization dedicated to advancing the humanities and learning. Established in 1960, the Foundation primarily provides grants for high-quality research within the humanities and social sciences, often prioritizing projects that integrate digital technology or explore the intersection of culture and society. By funding individual researchers, innovative academic projects, and cultural preservation initiatives, the Foundation plays a vital role in deepening historical and social understanding while enriching the broader European cultural landscape.
