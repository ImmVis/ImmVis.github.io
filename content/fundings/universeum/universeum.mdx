Universeum is Sweden’s national science center and a major public hub for science, technology, and sustainable development based in Gothenburg. Across its multi-floor facility, which includes a rainforest, a massive aquarium, and advanced digital laboratories, the center focuses on sparking curiosity and deepening public knowledge in STEM fields. As a core strategic partner, Universeum collaborates with academic institutions and tech organizations to host state-of-the-art interactive spaces—such as the digital VisLab—allowing visitors of all ages to explore complex data and cutting-edge scientific research firsthand.
