The Swedish e-Science Research Centre (SeRC) is a leading national initiative focused on developing cutting-edge digital tools and data-driven methodologies to solve complex scientific challenges. Founded as a strategic collaboration between KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm University, Karolinska Institutet, and Linköping University, SeRC bridges the gap between advanced computational infrastructure and applied research. By combining high-performance computing, data science, and artificial intelligence with expertise in fields ranging from climate modeling to molecular life sciences, the Centre serves as a vital hub for interdisciplinary innovation, driving scientific breakthroughs that benefit both academia and society.
