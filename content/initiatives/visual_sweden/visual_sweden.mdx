# Overview

<Image float="right" width="40%" src="VR_Tech Day 2024.jpg" alt="VR Tech Day 2024" />

Visual Sweden is an innovation initiative based in the Linköping-Norrköping region, recognized as one of Europe’s premier hubs for visual computing and image analysis. Operating as a central node in Östergötland’s smart specialization strategy, the organization unifies regional municipalities, Linköping University, government agencies (such as SMHI, NFC, and FOI), and over 100 private enterprises to turn cutting-edge visual research into market-ready applications.

Rather than conducting isolated research, Visual Sweden operates as a collaborative catalyst—matching technological breakthroughs with market challenges, orchestrating matchmaking forums, and providing targeted innovation grants to accelerate digital transformation.

## Core Areas of Application

The platform focuses its innovation stimulus on driving sustainable developments across three main application areas:

- **Industry:** Enhancing automated quality control and process monitoring through advanced camera surveillance, computer vision, and predictive aftermarket maintenance models.
- **Medicine:** Advancing precision tools for digital pathology, orthopedics, and virtual autopsies, alongside telemetry systems built to support remote, population-level diagnostics.
- **Society:** Powering digital twin technologies for smart urban planning, remote air traffic control systems, interactive educational software, and sensor fusion for crime scene visualization.
