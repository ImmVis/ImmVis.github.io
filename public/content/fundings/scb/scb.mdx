Statistics Sweden (SCB) is the official government agency responsible for developing, producing, and disseminating national statistics to support decision-making, debate, and research. Operating under the Ministry of Finance, the agency systematically gathers high-quality data across all sectors of society, including demographics, economics, housing, and education. As a trusted data partner, SCB provides the foundational, objective metrics necessary for organizations to map societal trends and translate complex national data into meaningful, public-facing insights.
