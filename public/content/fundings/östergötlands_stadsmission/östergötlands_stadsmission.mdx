Östergötlands Stadsmission is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting vulnerable individuals and families, combating social exclusion, and fostering a more inclusive society across the Östergötland region. Operating independently but in cooperation with local municipalities and churches, the organization provides open social work, shelter, counseling, and empowering youth leadership programs. As a community-focused funder and partner, Östergötlands Stadsmission actively collaborates on innovative initiatives that amplify the voices of underrepresented groups, helping to bridge the gap between academic research and local societal impact.
