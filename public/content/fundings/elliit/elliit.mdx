ELLIIT is a premier Swedish Strategic Research Environment dedicated to securing the nation's position at the global forefront of information technology and mobile communications. A powerful alliance between Linköping University, Lund University, Halmstad University, and Blekinge Institute of Technology, the center bridges the gap between fundamental research and industrial application. By focusing on critical future domains like 6G networks, artificial intelligence, and cyber-physical systems, ELLIIT drives interdisciplinary innovation, attracts top international talent, and equips Swedish industry with cutting-edge expertise.
