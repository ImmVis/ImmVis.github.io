Vetenskapsrådet is Sweden's largest governmental research funding body, dedicated to ensuring the nation remains a global leader in scientific discovery and academic excellence. Operating under the Ministry of Education and Research, the Council distributes significant funding each year across all scientific disciplines, from medicine and engineering to the humanities and social sciences. Beyond allocating grants through a rigorous, competitive peer-review process, Vetenskapsrådet serves as a key advisory body to the Swedish government on research policy, funds critical national and international research infrastructures, and works extensively to foster science communication and open access within the global research community.
