Getinge is a leading global provider of products and systems that contribute to quality enhancement and cost efficiency within healthcare and life sciences. Founded in Sweden in 1904, the company specializes in intensive care, cardiovascular procedures, operating rooms, sterile reprocessing, and life science applications. Operating with a presence in over 40 countries, Getinge focuses on technological innovation and clinical excellence, collaborating with academic and tech partners to pioneer next-generation solutions, advanced training tools, and interactive visual platforms that support healthcare professionals worldwide.
