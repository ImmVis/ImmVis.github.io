Smart Built Environment is a Swedish national Strategic Innovation Program dedicated to spearheading the digital transformation within the built environment sector. Funded jointly by Vinnova, Formas, and the Swedish Energy Agency, the program supports research, development, and innovation aimed at creating a more sustainable, resource-efficient, and integrated approach to urban planning and construction. By funding forward-looking initiatives that leverage advanced data visualization, geographic information systems, and digital twin technology, Smart Built Environment accelerates the transition toward data-driven, smarter cities and paves the way for the future of sustainable urban management.
