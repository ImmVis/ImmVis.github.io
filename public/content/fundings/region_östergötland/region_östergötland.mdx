Region Östergötland is the regional government authority responsible for healthcare, public transport, and regional development in the Östergötland province. Dedicated to driving sustainable growth and enhancing the region's competitiveness, the authority actively funds and supports initiatives that bridge the gap between academic research, public enterprise, and industrial innovation. As a vital strategic funder, Region Östergötland invests in major regional clusters and technological programs—such as Visual Sweden, Immersive Sweden, and Visual Lift—to foster a thriving ecosystem for advanced visualization, digitalization, and regional collaboration.
