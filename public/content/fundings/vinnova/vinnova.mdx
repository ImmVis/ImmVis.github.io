Vinnova is Sweden’s innovation agency, a government authority operating under the Ministry of Climate and Enterprise with the vital mission of strengthening the nation's innovative capacity and promoting sustainable growth. As a key catalyst for systemic change, Vinnova injects significant funding annually into research and development projects across various sectors, including digital transformation, future mobility, health, and deep tech. Rather than just funding isolated projects, the agency focuses on mobilizing collaborative networks that bring together industry, academia, the public sector, and civil society to tackle pressing societal challenges, experiment with new ideas, and elevate Sweden's global competitiveness in a sustainable world.
