# Overview

Wisdome is a national initiative that unites five of Sweden’s leading science centers to revolutionize how we perceive and understand the world.

By combining cutting-edge immersive technology with state-of-the-art dome theaters, Wisdome makes the invisible visible. It takes audiences to places otherwise impossible to visit—from the deepest depths of the oceans and the microscopic inner workings of the human body, to the farthest edges of the known universe.

### The Five Wisdome Sites

The initiative forms a powerful national network spanning across Sweden:

* **Visualiseringscenter C** (Norrköping)
* **Universeum** (Gothenburg)
* **Tekniska museet** (Stockholm)
* **Malmö Museer** (Malmö)
* **Curiosum** (Umeå)
