Projects

Here is a list of projects and activities that the group are undertaking. These can involved larger software development projects or small, contained, research projects and everything in between. Each project on this webpage contains more information to show who worked on it, who funded the project, and additionally provides supplemental material that explains what that project is about, links to software archives, etc.

Analyzing Motion in Dome Films Using Optic Flow

2025 — ongoing

Using optic flow to analyze motion in dome films, and its implications for visual perception and motion sickness in the context of dome production.

TellUs

2025 — ongoing

An interactive visualization globe utilizing AI to engage primary students in STEM through conversational exploration of Earth and space data.

C-Play : Immersive Media Player

2021 — ongoing

A versatile media player for cluster environments, enabling synchronized playback of immersive formats like 360°, fisheye, and stereoscopic video.

ExoplanetExplorer in OpenSpace

2021 — ongoing

Interactive Contextualized Exploration of Exoplanet Data for Experts.

UTM City

2016 — ongoing

Increase our understanding of unmanned traffic in cities, a future situation, and to explore new concepts for understanding and controlling traffic.

OpenSpace

2013 — ongoing

An open-source interactive data visualization software designed to explore the entire known universe using real-time space mission data.

SimStad

2022 — 2026

An urban planning platform and digital twin using AI to visualize city data for maintenance, simulation, and public outreach.

Data & Me

2024 — 2025

A collaborative exhibition exploring personal data visualization as a tool for self-empowerment, expression, and humanistic community storytelling.

Tracing Carbon

2021 — 2024

An interactive learning platform for high school students exploring the carbon cycle and systems thinking through adaptive, game-like exercises.

Getinge Dome Production

2023

A collaborative project between Linköping University and Getinge creating immersive 8K dome movies to showcase medical technology products.

Ecoweb

2020 — 2023

An interactive ecological simulation exploring biodiversity and species interdependence through real-time food web modeling and scientific data.

Havets näringsväv

2020 — 2023

An interactive visualization of the Weddell Sea food web using Unreal Engine to explore Antarctic biodiversity and ecosystem fragility.

Sweden in Numbers

2020 — 2023

An interactive demographic visualization exploring Swedish longitudinal population data from 1990–2017, focusing on income, education, and migration trends.

Klimatmat

2021 — 2022

An interactive installation where users assemble meals to visualize the real-time environmental impact of different food choices.

The Globe

2021 — 2022

An interactive ecological simulation exploring biodiversity and species interdependence through real-time food web modeling and scientific data.

Under the surface

2021

An educational visualization project preserving a decade of unique marine biodiversity data collected from Sweden's first underwater national park.
